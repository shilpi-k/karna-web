Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

KarNa ("we", "us", or "our") is a personal task management app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We believe in plain language — if anything is unclear, email us.

1. Information We Collect

When you use KarNa, we collect:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Tasks, goals, and shopping items — the content you add to the app
  • Voice recordings — when you use voice input; recordings are transcribed and not stored after processing
  • Routine and preferences — your daily schedule and focus areas
  • Notification preferences — your push notification settings
  • Device push token — to deliver notifications to your device
  • Usage data — product analytics events (e.g. "task created") linked to your account, to understand how features are used

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and sync your tasks, goals, and shopping lists across sessions
  • To power AI features: auto-filling task details, generating daily summaries, and suggesting scheduling windows
  • To send push notifications for task reminders
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the app using anonymised usage patterns

3. AI Processing — Third-Party Subprocessors

KarNa's AI features send your content to the following third-party services. By using AI features you consent to this processing.

  • Anthropic (Claude) — processes task titles and your routine text to infer due dates, urgency, categories, and generate daily summaries. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI (Whisper) — processes voice recordings to produce text transcriptions. Audio is sent to OpenAI's API and is not retained after transcription. OpenAI Privacy Policy

Neither Anthropic nor OpenAI uses your data to train their models under our API agreements.

4. Infrastructure and Other Services

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS). Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — error monitoring. Captures anonymised crash reports to help us fix bugs. No task content is included in error reports. Sentry Privacy Policy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery for support confirmations. Resend Privacy Policy
  • Expo (EAS) — app delivery and over-the-air updates. Expo Privacy Policy
  • PostHog — product analytics. We link usage events to your account (via your user ID, name, and email) to understand how features are used. Task, goal, and shopping content is not sent to PostHog. PostHog Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts — your data is retained as long as your account exists
  • Completed tasks — kept in our database after completion for future features (e.g. progress tracking); not visible in the app after 24 hours
  • Purchased shopping items — permanently deleted from our database 24 hours after purchase
  • Deleted goals — permanently deleted from our database 24 hours after deletion
  • Deleted accounts — your account is deactivated immediately upon deletion. All associated data (tasks, goals, shopping items) is permanently and irrecoverably deleted within 30 days

6. Deleting Your Account

To delete your KarNa account:

  • Open the app and go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Optionally tell us why you're leaving
  • Confirm — you can also request deletion by emailing shilpikarn@gmail.com

What happens to your data:

  • Your account is deactivated and hidden from the app immediately
  • All associated data — tasks, goals, shopping items, routine, and preferences — is permanently and irrecoverably deleted from our database within 30 days
  • You can also delete individual tasks, goals, or shopping items at any time without deleting your whole account

7. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect payment information (no paid features currently)
  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

8. Admin Access

As the operator of KarNa, we have technical access to data stored in our database. We commit to accessing individual user data only when you contact us for support and explicitly request assistance. We do not browse user task or goal content.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — see "Deleting Your Account" above
  • Correction — update your name in the app under Settings, or update your preferences under Settings → Preferences
  • Portability — request an export of your data by emailing us
  • Objection — object to processing by contacting us

10. Children's Privacy

KarNa is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of KarNa after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
